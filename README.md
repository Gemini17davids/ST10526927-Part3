1.1 Brief History 

The creation of a new footbridge boosts the economy in the world in rural and urban developing countries. BRIDGE OF LIFE is an organization that helps people in the community. It was created in 2026 to help the community to provide the needs of the people. We saw a gap that the government did not fill, and our organization is here to fill that gap. The organization works with the community to make life feel better for everyone; we believe everyone is important and deserves love, respect, and support. We are here to bring light, build a better future for everyone, and life will improve one step at a time 

After few weeks the Bridge of Life had earned national and international awards, including key to the nation in South Africa. It is a proud member of the South African Service Provider. 

1.2 Mission Statemen 

To provide health care services in developing areas worldwide. Build hospitals, clinics and mobile clinics and open job opportunities, hire and train local staff. Delivers health care to the community, treatment and indoor services. We also provide technical knowledge making sure all the people know and understand how to use technology so that it could be easy to access anything in the world that needs technology. 

1.3 Vision Statement 

To create an accessible platform that connects people with the organization’s mission, activities, and impact. It serves as a digital site where supporters, volunteers, and sponsors can engage, share ideas, and work with us to create a better future for our communities. By building and publishing the website, we are building it to show our success stories and opportunities for involvement. The website trust strengthens community bonds and inspires action, and it aims to create a lasting positive it serves. 

1.4 Target Audience 

1. People and Countries who are in crises 

2. People, countries, province who call for help and who have less material to survive

Accessibility – clean colour contrast, clean images and buttons 

Page load speed – Target 2.3 seconds, slow image loading 

Trust signals – Security badges and guest testimonials 

Enquiry – streamlined 2-step enquiring process: Fill the form > write your message and submit 

Navigation – Two clicks in any page, simple navigation
